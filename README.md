# Sales-Analysis-using-Python-EDA-

Objective
Performed exploratory data analysis on Diwali sales data to understand customer behavior and sales patterns.

Tools: Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn

Objective
1.Cleaned and preprocessed raw data by handling missing values, incorrect data types, and outliers to ensure accurate analysis.
2.Analyzed sales based on gender, age group, occupation, and product category to identify high-value customer segments and best-selling products.
3.Created insightful data visualizations using Matplotlib and Seaborn to support marketing and inventory planning decisions.

Outcome
most of the buyers are of age group between 26-35 yrs female.
